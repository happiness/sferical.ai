Building the future AI infrastructure for Swedish Industry

Sferical AI is co-founded by AstraZeneca, Ericsson, Saab, SEB and Wallenberg Investments. We are establishing a sovereign AI compute platform in Sweden, powered by NVIDIA’s latest DGX SuperPOD technology, to give our partner companies secure, large-scale access to world-class AI compute.

Our goal is to help partners turn AI into measurable business impact. With state-of-the-art compute capacity and shared industrial expertise, Sferical will enable faster R&D, more efficient engineering workflows and support the development of new AI-driven capabilities and applications.

Built in Sweden and shaped by leading global companies, we focus on delivery, reliability and long-term value creation. We work closely with partners to test, build and scale solutions that strengthen their competitiveness in a rapidly evolving landscape.
